ABOUT GYMMBOXX

Established in October 2010, GYMMBOXX started as a single gym located at the top of a multi-storey car park in Bedok Reservoir. Over the years, GYMMBOXX has made its mark as Singapore's affordable premium gym brand, serving the residential neighbourhoods and heartlands. With more than 7 gyms islandwide, GYMMBOXX will continue to bring quality gym facilities and service to the rest of Singapore and beyond.

WHY JOIN GYMMBOXX?

GYMMBOXX welcomes the public to visit our gym without having to purchase a membership plan.

We believe in providing everyone access to quality gym equipment, facilities, and services without imposing a long membership plan.

Our pay-per-entry allows the public to have full access to all GYMMBOXX, which includes the 24-hours gym, on a visit-by-visit basis.
